Ingredients:
 - 1 brick ramen
 - 2 cups water
 - 1 flavor packet

Directions:
 1. Heat water in small saucepan until boiling

 2. Break ramen into a few smaller bricks and place in water for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally
 
 3. Remove pan from heat and add flavoring to pan. Stir until flavoring is dissolved. Season to taste
